Wood Chippings are a drop from hammering wood logs with a normal Ex Nihilo hammer.
One log of wood will drop a random amount of 1-4 Wood Chippings.

## Usage

Wood Chippings can be put into an Ex Nihilo Barrel to be composted into dirt.
