### How to use the Heating Unit on Fabric?

The Heating Unit is currently not supported on Fabric.

This issue is being [tracked on GitHub](https://github.com/TwelveIterationMods/Balm/issues/67).

### Is Cooking for Blockheads compatible with ... ?

Cooking for Blockheads is compatible with most mods that add food items by default.
