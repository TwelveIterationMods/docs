The sharestone is a block that can be used to teleport to other sharestones of the same color. 

Unlike waystones, sharestones do not require the player to activate them first.

All sharestones are always available to all players, making them a great tool for multiplayer servers.

### Usage

-   Right-click a sharestone to choose a sharestone of the same color to teleport to.
-   Shift-right-click a sharestone to edit its name.
