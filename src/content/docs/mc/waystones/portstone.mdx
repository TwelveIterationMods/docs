Portstones are essentially one-way waystones. 

You can use them to teleport to any other activated [Waystone](waystone) or colored sharestone matching its color, but they can not be teleported to themselves.

Only the white portstone can be used to teleport to regular waystones, all other portstones are linked to their respective sharestones instead.

### Usage

- Right-click a portstone to open the waystone selection screen.
- Select a waystone to teleport to it.
