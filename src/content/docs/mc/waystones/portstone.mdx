The port stone is like a one-way waystone. You can use it to teleport to any other activated [Waystone](waystone), but it can not be teleported to itself.

---

### Crafting

<CraftingRecipe recipe="waystones:portstone"/>

### Usage

- Right-click a portstone to open the waystone selection screen.
- Select a waystone to teleport to it.
