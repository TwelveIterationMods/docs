Crumbling Attuned Shards are like regular [Attuned Shards](attuned_shard), except they are consumed once used in a [Warp Plate](warp_plate).

---

### Crafting

Crumbling attuned shards can be created within a Warp Plate by placing an ender pearl in the center and four Amethyst Shards around it.

### Usage

- Place the attuned shard from one Warp Plate into another Warp Plate's center slot.
